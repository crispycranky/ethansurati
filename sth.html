Scout Trooper Helmet

The iconic Scout Trooper helmet from Star Wars was a multi-day project—not just for the 3D printing but also due to the extensive sanding, painting, and finding a visor that fit the helmet perfectly. I made the decals using regular paper, which I glued onto the helmet, and gave it a layered white gloss spray paint job with Krylon, combined with light black spray paint to simulate the dirt and wear seen in the film. The helmet opens and closes just like the one in the movie. My main inspiration for this project was simply that I wanted it but wasn’t willing to pay for it, so I decided to build it myself. I’d say it turned out pretty great!
