Early project showcasing growth in 3D printing and finishing techniques from Star Wars

Death Trooper Helmet

The Death Trooper helmet was one of the earliest projects I ever completed and marked my first attempt at creating a helmet using a 3D printer. I printed it on my old Ender 3 printer and, like my later projects, it involved sanding and smoothing multiple pieces to achieve a uniform finish.

As a first attempt, I would say the project turned out decent, but it's clear that the quality of my work has significantly improved in my subsequent projects, as you can see from the newer builds.

Project Significance

This helmet represents an important milestone in my 3D printing journey. While it may not have the polish of my later work, it demonstrates the foundation of skills that I've continued to develop and refine over time. Every maker has to start somewhere, and this project shows where my journey began.
