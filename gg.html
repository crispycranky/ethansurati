Green Goblin's Mask

The Green Goblin mask was one of the most enjoyable projects I've completed. It was the final project I tackled on my old Ender 3 printer and required quite a bit of time to print all the individual pieces and assemble them. Due to the mask being printed in multiple parts, I employed extensive smoothing techniques to achieve a seamless finish. I also had the opportunity to experiment with a special type of color-changing paint, inspired by the mask seen in the 2002 Spider-Man film series. The end result turned out great—it’s fully wearable, and you can see through the lenses, which I repurposed from a sun visor.
