Green Goblin Mask

Wearable mask with color-changing paint and repurposed sun visor lenses for authentic visibility

Green Goblin's Mask

The Green Goblin mask was one of the most enjoyable projects I've completed. It was the final project I tackled on my old Ender 3 printer and required quite a bit of time to print all the individual pieces and assemble them.

Due to the mask being printed in multiple parts, I employed extensive smoothing techniques to achieve a seamless finish. I also had the opportunity to experiment with a special type of color-changing paint, inspired by the mask seen in the 2002 Spider-Man film series.

The end result turned out great—it's fully wearable, and you can see through the lenses, which I repurposed from a sun visor.

Project Highlights

• Multi-part assembly requiring extensive smoothing
• Color-changing paint effect inspired by the 2002 film
• Fully wearable with functional visibility
• Repurposed sun visor lenses for authentic look
• Final major project on Ender 3 printer
