TIE Fighter Pilot Helmet And Suit

Imperial pilot from Star Wars with accurate detailing and finish

About the Build

The TIE Fighter pilot helmet is one of the most iconic pieces of Imperial equipment in the Star Wars universe. This build captures the menacing presence of the Empire's elite starfighter pilots.

I had to outsource the boots, gloves and jumpsuit but everything else was made in house, You might notice one of the decals is higher than the other and it makes me mad

Key Features

• Screen-accurate Imperial pilot design
• Detailed breathing apparatus and hose connections
• Glossy black finish with gray accents
• Tinted visor for visibility

Build Challenges

The TIE pilot helmet presents unique challenges with its distinctive shape and multiple component parts that must fit together seamlessly to achieve an authentic look.
