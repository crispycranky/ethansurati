Halo: Reach Noble Six helmet with custom visor featuring intricate game-accurate details

Noble 6's Helmet

It's not every day you get to print an entire helmet, or so I thought as I set out to make the Noble Six helmet. Unfortunately, when it was all said and done, the helmet turned out unusually small. While it's still wearable, it's noticeably smaller than it should be and doesn't fit many heads.

Even so, I completed the project, including a custom visor featuring the intricate details seen in the game. Despite the effort, this helmet remains something of a reminder of a near-miss because of its scale.

For those unfamiliar, this helmet is from the video game Halo: Reach, where the main character, Noble Six, wears it throughout the campaign—assuming players haven't customized it away from the default look.

Lessons Learned

This project taught me valuable lessons about scaling and measurement in 3D printing. While the final product didn't turn out exactly as planned in terms of size, it still showcases attention to detail and commitment to completing the project despite challenges.
