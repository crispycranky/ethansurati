NCR Ranger Helmet

Iconic helmet from Fallout: New Vegas with weathered wasteland finish

About the Build

The NCR Ranger helmet is one of the most recognizable pieces of armor from Fallout: New Vegas. This project captures the post-apocalyptic aesthetic of the Mojave Wasteland with careful attention to weathering and detail.

Key Features

• Authentic wasteland weathering effects
• Game-accurate proportions and details
• Custom paint job with distressing
