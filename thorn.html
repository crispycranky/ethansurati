Thorn Hand Cannon

Life-size replica of the iconic Destiny hand cannon with working cylinder and ammunition storage

What is the Thorn?

The Thorn is a hand cannon in the games Destiny and Destiny 2, known for its unique, otherworldly look. Since I spent a lot of time playing these games, I decided to create a real-life re-creation of this iconic endgame item.

As you can see in the image, Thorn has an almost ethereal appearance that sets it apart. Unfortunately, I don't have many in-progress photos, as this was a quick, one-day build. The revolving cylinder can be opened, and a pseudo-ammunition cartridge can be stored within it.

Project Details

This build showcases my ability to rapidly prototype and create detailed replicas from popular gaming franchises. The attention to detail and functional elements like the working cylinder demonstrate both technical skill and creative problem-solving.
