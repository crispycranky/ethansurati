Halo Reach Assault Rifle

The Halo assault rifle was the first large project I completed on my current 3D printer. I used a combination of painting techniques and adhesion methods to ensure the finished product was both stable and visually appealing. This full-size replica of the assault rifle from the video game Halo: Reach even includes a removable magazine and a stand.
