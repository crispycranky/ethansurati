About Me

I graduated from Indiana State University in December 2024 with a degree in Data Sciences. As a first-generation American with parents from abroad, I take pride in my diverse background and bring unique perspectives to every project.

My professional journey began as a fullstack web developer at GRO, where I honed my skills across the entire development stack. I've since transitioned to focus on back-end development, working with CSS, HTML, JavaScript, and back-end DBMS applications including SQL and NoSQL.

Beyond coding, I'm an avid collector of historical arms and armor, including 14th-century knight armor and 1500s Japanese samurai replicas. I enjoy 3D printing props and helmets from my favorite games and movies, video gaming, and airsoft.
